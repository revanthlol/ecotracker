Why Track Your Carbon Footprint?

Your carbon footprint is the total amount of greenhouse gases produced by your daily activities. Understanding and tracking these emissions is the first step toward reducing them.

How It Works

1. Log your daily activities across different categories

2. View your total emissions and identify high-impact areas

3. Make informed decisions to reduce your environmental impact

Tips for Reduction

• Use public transportation or bike when possible

• Reduce meat consumption and food waste

• Choose energy-efficient appliances

• Support sustainable products and services
